# Code of Conduct

This project uses a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Stay on the technical topic.
- Do not post secrets, personal data, abusive content, or illegal-use instructions.
- Do not use issues or discussions to promote public operation, resale, proxy, VPN, bypass, evasion, or other out-of-scope services.

Maintainers may close, edit, hide, or remove content that is abusive, unsafe, illegal, off-topic, or outside the project scope.
